Ercan Airport Database Management System
Project Overview Welcome to my CMPE343 Database Systems project. For this assignment, I designed and implemented a relational database to manage the technical and personnel operations of Ercan Airport. The goal was to move away from messy spreadsheets and create a "Single Source of Truth" for airport managers, technicians, and safety inspectors.

What This Database Does I built this system to handle three main areas of airport life:

The Team: Tracking every employee, from the high-stakes Air Traffic Controllers (and their medical exam dates) to the specialized Technicians.

The Fleet: Managing the actual airplanes, their blueprints (PlaneModels), and exactly where they are parked (Hangar Tracking).

The Safety: A rigorous logging system that tracks safety tests, which technician performed them, and the resulting scores.
